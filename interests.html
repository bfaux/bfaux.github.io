My Interests

I am constantly reading news, websites, magazines, blogs and anything I come across detailing the influence of popular culture. I go to see live theater both professional and amateur several times a year. I love discovering new music artists, films, and books by listening to various podcasts. The podcasts I listen to the most are Pop Culture Happy Hour, NPR Fresh Air, NPR Tiny Desk Concerts, and The Dissolve Podcast. I am a music lover, singer, and ocassional lyricist. After graduation I hope to begin a career where I can write about the things I find most interesting. Here are a few of my favorite websites/blogs that sum up what I hope to be doing as a journalist post-graduation.

  • Theater-Broadway.com
  • Film-The Dissolve
  • Monkey See NPR-Pop Culture Happy Hour
